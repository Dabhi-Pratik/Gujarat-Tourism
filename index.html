Pick Your Trail - See Gujarat - As Per Your Interest

Dwarka

An ancient and prosperous city believed to have been the capital of Lord Krishna's kingdom, Dwarka is an important pilgrimage centre for Hindus from all over the world. It is located on the western coast of Gujarat, on the banks of River Gomti.
Adi_kadi

Adi-kadi Vav, built in the 15th century, is carved entirely out of hard rock. A narrow flight of 120 stairs cuts down through the stone to meet the well shaft deep in the stone. Two different legends claim to explain the name of the well.
